Younes Bensouda Mourri

Teaches artificial intelligence at Stanford University and advocates for AI education. Wants to help people overcome the coming wave of job automation.

More About Me

Younes was born and raised in Morocco and advocates for Artficial Intelligence education. He helps people learn new skills to prepare them for jobs in a fast changing world.

Prefers working on problems that do not have easy solutions.

His work focuses on Artificial Intelligence. Younes prefers working with products from the start. He is interested in data acquisition strategies to build solid AI models during the early prototyping phases of product.

Finally, while at Stanford, he built an interest in education technology and is particularly interested in using technology and AI for better social learning.

What I Work On

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is changing the way the world works. Many people are not aware of the countless jobs this field is/and is about to automate. Designs models that save humans the pain of doing repetitive tasks on a daily basis.

AI education

Learning something and not sharing the know-how is one of society's biggest crimes. In his free time, Younes builds Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) to share the latest state-of-the-art AI techniques with as many people as possible.

Education Technology

Throughout the last couple of years Younes has collaborated with institutions like Stanford, Coursera and deeplearning.ai. He used efficient online learning to contribute to building smart ed-tech solutions.

Research

Strongly believes in an AI powered education. What if teachers can assign homework by learning objective and not just a fixed set of problems?
